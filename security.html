Client Asset Protection

Defense-Grade Security.

At INGRO Global, we believe security is not an option it is the foundation of our firm. Since we operate without a public office, we invest 100% of our security budget into digital vaulting and private account protection.

Fund Isolation

Your money is never mixed with the company’s daily operating cash. Every client’s capital is held in a "segregated" private account, meaning it is only ever used for your specific investment plan.

End-to-End Encryption

Every piece of data you enter from your name to your withdrawal requests is protected by 256-bit SSL encryption. This is the same level of security used by global military organizations.

Withdrawal Verification

To prevent unauthorized access, every withdrawal request requires multi-step approval. No money leaves the system without a confirmed verification from your registered secure device.

Our Three Pillars of Digital Safety.

1. Zero-Knowledge Infrastructure

We do not store your passwords in plain text. Our "Zero-Knowledge" system means that even if a technician were to look at our servers, they could not see your access codes. Only you hold the key to your account.

2. Real-Time Threat Monitoring

Our systems are monitored 24/7 by automated security protocols. If our system detects a login attempt from an unusual location or a new device, it instantly locks the account until you personally unlock it.

3. Capital Reserve Guarantee

We maintain an "Insurance Fund" that is held in physical liquid cash. This fund is designed to protect our clients' principal investment against extreme market events or technical failures.
